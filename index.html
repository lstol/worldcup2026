🏆 Welcome to World Cup 2026!

This is the Stoltenberg family prediction competition for the 2026 FIFA World Cup in the USA, Canada and Mexico. Predict match scores across all 104 games, compete against family members, and see who comes out on top when the final whistle blows on July 19!

📅 Tournament runs June 11 – July 19, 2026  •  48 teams  •  104 matches  •  12 groups

📋 Rules

⚽
Predict the score of every group stage match before it kicks off. Enter home and away goals for each game.
🎯
Exact score = full points. Correct result (win/draw/loss) but wrong scoreline = partial points. Wrong result = 0 points.
🔒
Knockout rounds unlock one at a time. Lasse will open each round — you'll get a notification when predictions are open.
💾
Autosave — predictions save automatically a few seconds after you stop typing. No need to press save.
⏰
Deadline — predictions for each match lock when it kicks off. Make sure to predict before the first whistle!
🏆
Winner — the player with the most points after the final on July 19 wins the bragging rights!
